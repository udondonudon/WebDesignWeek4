Studio Album Album Art Relase Date and Singles
Bleach Release Date: June 15, 1989
Singles: Love Buzz and Blew
Nirvana’s debut album, Bleach was recorded in Seattle, Washington over the course of multiple months before releasing in June of 1989. Selling 40k copies, the album has been certified platinum and ranked 13th on Rolling Stone’s 50 Greatest Grunge Albums.
Nevermind Release Date: September 24, 1991
Singles: Smells Like Teen Spirit, On a Plain, Come as You Are, Lithium, and In Bloom
The follow-up studio album, Nevermind initially had low sales expectations, however the album has sold over 30 million copies worldwide, becoming one of the best-selling albums ever. Certified diamond, this album has been included in the National Recording Registry by the Library of Congress.
In Utero Release Date: September 21, 1993
Singles: Heart-Shaped Box, All Apologies, and Pennyroyal Tea
Considered to be a drastic departure and in a vastly different style than the prior album, In Utero was recorded quickly in February 1993, releasing in September of the same year. Again certifying platinum, this album has sold 15 million copies across the globe.
